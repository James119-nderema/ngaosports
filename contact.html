Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register for activities?

You can register by filling out the contact form above or visiting our facility during working hours. Our team will guide you through the registration process.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept participants of all ages! We have programs tailored for children, teenagers, and adults across all our activities.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is needed. We have programs for beginners as well as advanced levels. Our coaches will assess your level and place you accordingly.

What are the fees?

Fees vary depending on the activity and program duration. Contact us for detailed pricing information and any available discounts or scholarships.
