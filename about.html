Our Story

Founded with a passion for sports and community development, Ngao Sports has been nurturing talents and building champions since our inception. Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality sports training and the opportunity to discover their potential.

Today, we are proud to offer a diverse range of activities including Football, Basketball, Skating, Robotics, Chess, and Music. Each program is designed to foster skill development, teamwork, and personal growth.
