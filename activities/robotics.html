About Our Robotics Program

Step into the exciting world of robotics where creativity meets technology. Our robotics program offers hands-on experience in building, programming, and operating robots. From simple machines to complex automated systems, students explore the fundamentals of engineering and computer science.

Our program is designed to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork. Students work on real projects, participate in competitions, and learn skills that prepare them for future careers in STEM fields.

What You'll Learn

  • Robot design and construction
  • Programming basics (Python, Scratch, Arduino)
  • Electronics and circuits
  • Sensors and automation
  • 3D printing and prototyping
  • Competition preparation

Program Levels

Beginner

Introduction to robotics with simple builds and basic programming concepts.

Intermediate

Advanced builds with sensors, motors, and more complex programming.

Advanced

Competition-level robotics with autonomous systems and AI integration.
