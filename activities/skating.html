About Our Skating Program

Discover the joy of skating with our comprehensive program that covers both ice skating and roller skating. From your first glide to advanced tricks and performances, our skilled instructors will guide you through every step of your skating journey.

Our skating program emphasizes proper technique, safety, and creative expression. Whether you're interested in recreational skating, figure skating, or roller sports, we have a program tailored for you.

What You'll Learn

  • Balance and posture fundamentals
  • Forward and backward skating
  • Stopping and turning techniques
  • Jumps and spins (figure skating)
  • Tricks and stunts (roller skating)
  • Performance and choreography

Program Levels

Beginner

Learn to skate safely with basic techniques and confidence building.

Intermediate

Develop more advanced skills including turns, crossovers, and basic tricks.

Advanced

Master complex moves, choreography, and prepare for performances.
